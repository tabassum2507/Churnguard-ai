---
title: "Troubleshooting Mobile App Sync Issues"
category: troubleshooting
---

If the FlowMetric mobile app isn't showing your latest data, pull down on the main screen to force a manual refresh first. The app syncs automatically on a fifteen-minute cycle, so if you just made changes on desktop they may not have appeared yet.

If a manual refresh doesn't help, check your connection settings. The app needs an active internet connection and won't sync if you've restricted background data for FlowMetric in your phone's settings. On iPhone, go to Settings, then FlowMetric, and confirm Background App Refresh is enabled. On Android, check Battery Optimization and set FlowMetric to unrestricted — aggressive battery savers on some Android manufacturers kill background network activity.

If the app is stuck on a loading spinner, force-closing and reopening it usually clears a stalled state. For a more thorough reset, sign out and sign back in, which triggers a full re-sync. This can take a minute or two on large workspaces, so give it time before concluding it isn't working.

Clearing the app cache before signing back in is worth doing on Android, as stale cache entries occasionally cause sync loops.
