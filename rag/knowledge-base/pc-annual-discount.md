---
title: "FlowMetric Annual Billing Discount"
category: plan_comparison
---

Switching to annual billing on FlowMetric saves you the equivalent of two months compared to paying month to month, which works out to about sixteen percent off across all paid plans. For Starter that's a saving of four thousand rupees a year. For Pro it's sixteen thousand rupees. Enterprise annual pricing is negotiated as part of your contract.

You can switch to annual billing at any time from the Billing section of your workspace settings. If you switch partway through a monthly billing cycle, the remaining days are prorated and applied as credit toward your first annual payment, so you won't be double-charged.

Annual plans renew automatically, and you'll get a reminder email thirty days before your renewal date so there are no surprises. If your team grows and you need to upgrade your plan mid-year, the cost difference is prorated for the remaining months of your annual term.

There are no cancellation penalties if you decide to leave during an annual term. Refunds for unused months are issued as account credit rather than back to the original payment method.
