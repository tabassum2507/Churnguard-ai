---
title: "Setting Up Automated Alerts in FlowMetric"
category: feature_tips
---

Automated alerts in FlowMetric mean you find out about problems before your stakeholders do. You can set up alerts for budget overruns — for example, get notified when a project hits eighty percent of its budget so you still have room to act. Milestone deadline alerts fire a configurable number of days before a due date, giving your team lead time to course-correct.

Engagement alerts are particularly useful for customer success teams. These fire when a customer's activity drops below a threshold you define, and they're what powers the churn detection workflow in ChurnGuard.

To create an alert, go to the Alerts section under Settings and click New Alert. You'll choose the trigger condition, the threshold value, and where to send the notification — email, Slack, or a webhook to your own system. All alerts fire in real time with no meaningful delay between the condition being met and the notification going out.

Alerts are available on Starter and above. The Free plan doesn't include alerting.
