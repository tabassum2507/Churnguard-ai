---
title: "Setting Up Your First Project in FlowMetric"
category: onboarding
---

Setting up your first project in FlowMetric takes about five minutes. After logging in, click New Project from the main dashboard and give it a name and a start date. The project type you choose shapes the default view: Task Board works well for software and product teams, Timeline suits marketing campaigns and events, and Resource Tracker is designed for professional services work where billable hours matter.

Once the project is created, you'll see a default set of columns based on the type you picked. Rename or add columns right away to match how your team actually works — there's no benefit to keeping the defaults if they don't fit your workflow.

The next step is adding milestones, which are the key dates your team is tracking. Even two or three milestones entered early give the reporting system something to measure against. Finally, invite at least one teammate before you close your first session. Projects come alive faster when more than one person is contributing, and your first report will be much more interesting with activity from multiple people on it.
