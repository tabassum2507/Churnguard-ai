---
title: "Team Collaboration Features in FlowMetric"
category: feature_tips
---

FlowMetric's collaboration features are built around the idea that everyone should always know what's happening without needing to ask. You can mention teammates directly inside task comments using the at-symbol, and they'll receive a notification by email or through your connected Slack channel. Shared views let you save a specific filter configuration and send the link to anyone on your workspace — what they see will match yours exactly, which is useful when you're talking through data on a call.

You can also leave comments on milestones and project boards, which works well for async teams spread across time zones. Guest access is available on Starter and above — guests can view and comment but cannot edit projects, making it a safe option for external stakeholders.

The team dashboard on Pro gives managers a live overview of every team member's workload and time logged. Cross-project reporting on Pro and Enterprise lets you pull multiple projects into a single view when teams share resources.
