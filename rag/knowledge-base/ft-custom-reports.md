---
title: "Building Custom Reports in FlowMetric"
category: feature_tips
---

Custom reports in FlowMetric let you slice your project data any way you need it. Start by going to the Reports section and clicking New Report. From there you choose which metrics to include — budget burn rate, time by team member, milestone completion rate, feature usage, or any combination of those. You can layer multiple filters on top of each other, so for example you could look at only enterprise-tier customers in a specific region over the last ninety days.

Once a report is configured the way you want it, save it to your dashboard so it's ready every time you log in. Reports are tied to your workspace, so the whole team can access any saved report you create, not just the person who built it.

Scheduled delivery is available on Pro and Enterprise. Set it up once and FlowMetric will email your report as a PDF or CSV on whatever cadence you choose — daily, weekly, or monthly — so stakeholders get fresh data without logging in.
