title: "FlowMetric Enterprise Features"
category: plan_comparison

FlowMetric Enterprise is built for organizations that need security controls, compliance tooling, and dedicated support on top of the standard product. The centerpiece is single sign-on — you can connect FlowMetric to your existing identity provider, whether that's Okta, Azure Active Directory, or Google Workspace. When someone leaves your company, their access to FlowMetric is revoked automatically when IT disables their account, with no manual steps required.

Enterprise also includes custom data retention policies, which matter for companies in regulated industries like finance, healthcare, or legal services. Audit logs on Enterprise go back two years, compared to ninety days on Pro, which satisfies most compliance and procurement requirements.

Your dedicated customer success manager handles onboarding, runs quarterly business reviews, and is your direct escalation path for any urgent issues. Custom API rate limits can be arranged if your integration needs exceed the standard thresholds.

Contracts can be written to match your procurement team's requirements, and multi-year pricing is available for organizations that want cost predictability. To start a conversation, reach out through your account manager or the Enterprise inquiry form on our website.
