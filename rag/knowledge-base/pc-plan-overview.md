---
title: "What Each FlowMetric Plan Includes"
category: plan_comparison
---

FlowMetric has four plans designed to grow with your team. The Free plan covers one active project and up to three users — it's a good fit for solo founders or very small teams just getting started with project tracking.

The Starter plan, at two thousand rupees a month, supports five projects and up to ten users. It adds data exports, basic reporting, and email support, and is where most early-stage teams land after outgrowing Free.

The Pro plan at eight thousand rupees a month is our most popular tier for growing companies. It removes the project and user limits, adds custom reports, automated alerts, full API access, scheduled exports, and the team workload dashboard.

Enterprise at twenty-five thousand rupees a month is designed for organizations that need single sign-on, custom data retention policies, audit logs going back two years, a dedicated customer success manager, and the ability to negotiate a custom contract with procurement-friendly terms.

All plans include a fourteen-day trial of Pro features when you first sign up, so you can explore the full product before choosing a tier.
