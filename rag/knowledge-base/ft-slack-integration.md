---
title: "Connecting FlowMetric to Slack"
category: feature_tips
---

Connecting FlowMetric to Slack takes about two minutes. Go to Settings, then Integrations, and click Connect next to Slack. You'll be redirected to authorize the FlowMetric app in your workspace, and once you return you choose which Slack channel each type of notification should go to. You can route milestone completions to your wins channel, budget alerts to your finance channel, and overdue task warnings to the relevant project channel — so the right people get the right messages.

The integration also supports slash commands. Type slash-flowmetric-status in any channel to get a quick summary of your active projects without leaving Slack. If you have multiple Slack workspaces, you can connect them all from the same settings page.

One thing worth knowing: notifications respect your quiet hours setting, so your team won't get pinged in the middle of the night unless you've explicitly disabled quiet hours. If notifications stop arriving, the most common fix is re-authorizing the connection from the Integrations page, as tokens can expire after a few months.
