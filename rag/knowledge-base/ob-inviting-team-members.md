---
title: "Inviting Team Members to FlowMetric"
category: onboarding
---

Inviting teammates to FlowMetric is straightforward. Go to Settings, then Team Members, and click Invite. Enter their email address and choose a role before sending. Admins can change workspace settings and manage billing. Members can create and edit projects. Viewers can see everything but cannot make changes, which is a good fit for stakeholders who need visibility without edit access.

Invites are valid for seventy-two hours. If a teammate doesn't accept in time, you can resend from the same panel — their invite will show as Pending until they click through.

If your company is on the Enterprise plan, you can skip manual invites by enabling SSO. Anyone who authenticates through your identity provider gets automatically added to your workspace with a default Member role, and access is revoked automatically when they leave the company.

For large team setups, you can bulk-import members by uploading a CSV file from the invite panel. Guest accounts for external collaborators are available on Starter and above, and guests don't count toward your plan's user limit. They can view and comment on projects you explicitly share with them.
