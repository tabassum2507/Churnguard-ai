---
title: "Troubleshooting Login Issues"
category: troubleshooting
---

If you're having trouble logging into FlowMetric, start with the most common cause: make sure you're using the exact email address tied to your account, not a forwarding alias. If you've forgotten your password, the reset link on the login page sends an email within about thirty seconds — check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

If your company uses single sign-on through Google Workspace or Microsoft 365, you need to use the SSO button on the login page, not the email and password form. Entering your password on the standard form won't work for SSO-enabled accounts.

Accounts get temporarily locked after five failed login attempts, and the lock lifts automatically after fifteen minutes. If you can't wait, a workspace admin can unlock your account from Team Settings under Members.

If you've recently changed your company email domain, reach out to support because your account likely needs to be migrated to the new address. Two-factor authentication problems are usually fixed by syncing the clock on your authenticator app, since TOTP codes are time-sensitive.
